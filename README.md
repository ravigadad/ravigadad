### Technical architect, systems thinker, and software engineer focused on modeling complex real-world systems into durable software platforms.

Over the last 20 years I’ve helped lead technical direction and foundational architecture at mission-driven startups spanning education, climate-focused fintech, workflow systems, and large-scale institutional data integration. My work has included designing source-of-truth data architectures, workflow/state-machine systems, and abstractions that allowed messy operational domains to become coherent, resilient software platforms. I’ve intentionally gravitated toward organizations tackling inequity in education and expanding access to energy efficiency and renewable energy funding.

I’m especially interested in systems that realign incentives, expand access, and challenge entrenched structures that create unnecessary friction, inequity, or extraction. Technically, I’m drawn to domain-driven design, human-centered systems, and architecture that helps people better understand and shape the systems they participate in.
